Privacy Policy

At Arrtytrails, we respect and protect your privacy. Your trust is our top priority, and we are committed to handling your personal information responsibly.

Information Collection

We collect only the information necessary to process your orders, provide updates, and ensure safe delivery.

This may include your name, contact details, billing/shipping address, and payment information.

Use of Information

Your details are used exclusively for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders.
  • Communicating important updates regarding your purchase.
  • Ensuring smooth and secure delivery.

Data Protection

All personal information is kept strictly confidential and stored securely.

We use safe practices to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

No Sharing Policy

We do not sell, share, or trade your personal information with any third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

Information is only shared with trusted delivery partners when required to complete your order.

Customer Rights

You have the right to request access, update, or removal of your personal information from our records at any time.

For any privacy-related concerns, you can contact us directly.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any changes will be communicated through our official platforms.
